Digital Literacy Project

This project is part of my Digital Literacy course where i worked as a Student Digital Ambassador. The main aim was to understand digital tools, safe internet practices, and how to build a professional online presence.

In this project i made an infographic, created my profiles on different platforms, tried coding practice, and made a quiz using Google Forms. I also learned about email etiquette and did a case study on cybercrime with a prevention checklist.

Overall, this project helped me understand how to use digital platforms better and now i feel more confident using them for academics and future use.

MODULE SUMMARIES

Module 1 – Digital Literacy Basics
In this module, I learned what digital literacy actually means and why it is important. I created an infographic covering useful tools, safe internet practices, and online communication.

Module 2 – Digital Portfolio
Here, I created my profiles on platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub. It helped me understand how to build a professional online presence from the start.

Module 3 – Platforms & Tools
In this module, I explored coding platforms like HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz. It helped me understand both technical practice and collaboration tools.

Module 4 – Email & Communication
This module focused on writing professional emails and understanding proper email etiquette. I also made a social media do’s and don’ts checklist.

Module 5 – Cybercrime Awareness
Here, I learned about online threats like UPI fraud. I wrote a case study and created a checklist to stay safe online.
